These are all the major exponent rules:
$$x^a\times x^b=x^{a+b}\\
\frac{x^a}{x^b}=x^{a-b}\\
(x^a)^b=x^{ab}\\
x^{-a}=\frac{1}{x^a}\\
x^0=1\\
x^{\frac{a}{b}}=\sqrt[b]{x^a}\\
x^a\times y^a=(xy)^a\\
\frac{x^a}{y^a}=(\frac{x}{y})^a
$$
