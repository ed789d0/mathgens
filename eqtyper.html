This is a simple tool that allows you to type equations using only special characters, which are supported by almost any app or website (such as Twitter/X, Discord, Facebook, WhatsApp, Zoom, etc). Just type your equation in the text box above (clicking the special character buttons when needed) and then click the "Copy" button to copy the equation to your clipboard. You can then paste it anywhere you like.
