$(r,\theta)$

-   r is directed distance

-   θ is angle

## Conversion


$x^{2} + y^{2} = r^{2}$, $x = r\cos\theta$, $y = r\sin\theta$

$\cos\theta = \frac{x}{r}$, $\sin\theta = \frac{y}{r}$,
$\tan\theta = \frac{y}{x}$

### Coordinates

#### R → P

$(\sqrt{x^2+y^2},arctan(\frac{y}{x}))$

#### P → R

$(rcos\theta,rsin\theta)$
